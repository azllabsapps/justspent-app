Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

JustSpent ("the app", "we", "our", or "us") is an offline expense-tracking application designed to help users manage their spending. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use

JustSpent does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data entered into the app (such as expense amounts or categories) is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to us or to any third parties.

Internet & Offline Use

JustSpent works entirely offline. The app does not require an internet connection and does not send any data externally.

Third-Party Services

JustSpent does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, your information remains under your control. We do not have access to any of the data stored within the app.

Children's Privacy

JustSpent is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
